2. SOC 2 TYPE II DETAILS

Our SOC 2 Type II audit covers all five Trust Services Criteria:
- Security: Comprehensive controls for protecting information and systems against unauthorized access.
- Availability: System availability commitments and monitoring (99.95% SLA).
- Processing Integrity: Controls to ensure system processing is complete, valid, and accurate.
- Confidentiality: Controls for protecting confidential information.
- Privacy: Controls for personal information collection, use, retention, and disposal.

The most recent audit period covered January 2024 through December 2024 with zero critical findings and two minor observations (both remediated within 30 days).

3. ISO 27001 SCOPE

Our ISO 27001 certification covers the design, development, operation, and maintenance of the ShieldSync cloud security posture management platform, including:
- All production infrastructure and environments
- Software development lifecycle processes
- Customer data handling and processing
- Physical and environmental security of office locations
- Human resources security processes
